How To Play

Each player will pick a team from column A, B, C and D then choose one of those teams to be a tiebreaker. Every team lineup is different so the earlier you pick your teams the more likely no one else will have that line up!

Points System

  • Win = 2 points
  • Tie = 1 points
  • Lose/Bye week = 0 points
